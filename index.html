Anxiety Exposure Tracker is designed with privacy at its core. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data. Everything stays on your device.

Overview

Anxiety Exposure Tracker ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our mobile application.

The simple version: We don't collect any data. Period. All your information stays on your device, and we have no access to it.

Information Collection and Use

Personal Data

We do NOT collect any personal information, including but not limited to:

  • Names or usernames
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Location data
  • Health or medical information
  • Photos or media
  • Contact lists
  • Device identifiers

Usage Data

We do NOT collect any usage data, analytics, or statistics about how you use the app.

Health Data

All health-related information you enter (exposure logs, anxiety levels, fear hierarchies, etc.) is stored locally on your device only. We have no servers, no cloud storage, and no way to access this information.

Data Storage

All data created within the app is stored locally on your device using Apple's standard storage systems. This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device
  • We cannot see, access, or retrieve your data
  • Your data is protected by your device's security features (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID)
  • If you delete the app, all data is permanently removed
  • Your data is included in your device's standard backups (iCloud or iTunes)

Third-Party Services

Anxiety Exposure Tracker does not integrate with any third-party services. We do not use:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • Crash reporting tools
  • Social media integrations
  • Cloud storage services

Data Sharing

Since we don't collect any data, we have nothing to share. We do not and cannot:

  • Sell your information
  • Share your information with third parties
  • Use your information for marketing
  • Transfer your information to other companies

Children's Privacy

Our app does not specifically target children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children or any other users because we don't collect information from anyone.

Data Security

Your data security is handled entirely by your device's built-in security features. We recommend:

  • Using a strong passcode on your device
  • Enabling Face ID or Touch ID
  • Keeping your iOS software updated
  • Using encrypted backups

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • Access: Open the app anytime to view your data
  • Edit: Modify any information directly in the app
  • Delete: Remove individual entries or delete the app to remove all data
  • Export: Use iOS screen recording or screenshots to save your data

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Since the app doesn't collect data or connect to the internet for updates, you may want to check this page occasionally.

Consent

By using Anxiety Exposure Tracker, you consent to this privacy policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use our application.

Medical Disclaimer

Anxiety Exposure Tracker is a self-help tool and is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always seek the advice of your physician or other qualified health provider with any questions you may have regarding a medical condition.
