Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with exposure therapy?
Start by creating your Fear Hierarchy in the Tools tab. List situations that cause anxiety, rating them from 1-10. Begin with lower-rated items and gradually work your way up. Always consult with a mental health professional if you're new to exposure therapy.
Is my data private and secure?
Yes! All your data is stored locally on your device only. We don't have servers, user accounts, or any way to access your information. When you delete the app, all data is permanently removed. Your privacy is absolute.
How do I track an exposure session?
  • Tap the "Log Exposure" tab
  • Enter a title and description
  • Start the timer when you begin
  • Rate your anxiety before, during (peak), and after
  • Record thoughts and physical sensations
  • Note which coping strategies you used
  • Save when complete
What's the difference between exposure types?
  • Situational: Real-world situations (stores, driving, crowds)
  • Interoceptive: Physical sensations (spinning, hyperventilating)
  • Imaginal: Imagining feared scenarios
  • Social: Social interactions and situations
How do I backup my data?
Your data is automatically included in your iPhone's regular backups (iCloud or iTunes/Finder). To ensure backups are enabled, go to Settings ‚Üí [Your Name] ‚Üí iCloud ‚Üí iCloud Backup and make sure it's turned on.
Can I export my data to share with my therapist?
Currently, you can take screenshots of your progress charts and exposure history to share. We're working on adding a proper export feature in a future update.
What if I'm having a mental health crisis?
If you're in crisis, please reach out for help immediately:
  • Emergency: Call 911 (US) or your local emergency number
  • Crisis Hotline: Call or text 988 (US)
  • Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741
  • International: Visit findahelpline.com
This app is a tool to support your journey, not a replacement for professional help.
How do I delete my data?
You can delete individual entries by swiping left on them in the History tab. To delete all data, simply delete the app from your device. All data is stored locally, so removing the app permanently erases everything.
Why isn't the app syncing across my devices?
The app doesn't sync because we don't use cloud storage - this is a privacy feature, not a limitation. All data stays on your device only. If you need the app on multiple devices, you'll need to track separately on each one.
Is this app a replacement for therapy?
No. This app is designed to complement professional treatment, not replace it. It's a tool to help track your exposure therapy exercises and progress. Always work with a qualified mental health professional for anxiety disorders.
